Western Adventure

A typical tour is our Western Adventure tour, featuring activities which can include white water rafting on the Colorado river, desert exploring, horse riding in the Wild West.

Borneo Adventure

A 15 day safari exploring the forests and beaches of Borneo. You will explore the forests of Kinabalu, climb the highest peak in South East Asia and come in close contact with the Orang Utan, the remarkable 'man of the forest'.
